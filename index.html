Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell domains?

Yes! The Personal, Premium, and developer plans include a free custom domain for one year. That includes new domains purchased through Vesperr.com or your own existing domain that you can map to your Vesperr.com site. Does not apply to premium domains. Domain name should be registered within one year of the purchase of the plan to use this promotion. Registered domain names will renew at regular prices. Find out more about domains.

Do I need another web host?

No. All Vesperr.com sites include our specially tailored Vesperr hosting to ensure your site stays available and secure at all times. You can even use your own domain when you upgrade to the Personal, Premium or developer plan.

Do you offer training?

Yes. We offer web-based training, in-person training and provide a myriad of free, high-quality educational materials. Please contact the Vesperr training team for more information.

Do you offer free web hosting?

Yes! We offer free web hosting. But limitations would differ according to the packages you choose. We have unlimited web hosting services in the developer plan.
